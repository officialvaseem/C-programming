Rules for naming variables or identifiers
* THEYHEY MUST BEGIN WITH A LETTER OR UNDERSCORE 
* THEY MUST ONLY CONSISTS OF LETTERS,DIGITS OR UNDERSCORE AND $. NO SPL CHAR IS ALOWED
*IT SHOULD NOT BE A KEYWORD
*IT MUST NOT CONTAIN A WHITE SPACE
*IT SHOULD BE UPTO 31 CHARACTERS LONG AS ONLY THE FIRST 31 CHARACTERS ARE SIGNIFICANT
